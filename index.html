About US

Our Objective

To bring public welfare to people domestically and internationally, to end destitution all together. This includes supporting and donating to the sick, distressed, disadvantaged and disable individuals and families, to relieve their suffering. We strive to support those affected from negligent, accidental and natural disasters, all from Canberra and other global regions.
